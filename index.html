About

As a Geographic Information Systems Specialist by trade (aka Cartographer) it's hard to deny my love for maps. A bit of a map nerd if you will. Hand me databases or spreadsheets, and I'll hand you beautiful data visualizations or a customly designed map. Other than mapping, I am currently enrolled in the Computer Science program at Union County College to cover the fundamentals before advancing into more specialized areas of informatics. The end goal is to combine my GIS skills with a full stack developer toolkit.
